ThalesCogent+0006

5 FBI Laboratory

The results of Section 5 are based on searches of the sequestered dataset FBI Laboratory. This dataset consists of 49 operational latent distal phalanx probes. Members of the FBI manually annotated the probe images and confirmed the ground truth mate. All probes searched were a single sample depicting a region from a distal phalanx. EFS data provided with the probe image may include:

  • Pattern classification
  • Minutia locations (unconfirmed source)
